Ingredients

  • 1/4 cup unsalted butter
  • 1/4 cup olive oil
  • 3 cups diced mushrooms
  • 1 cup chopped onion
  • 1 sprig rosemary
  • 1.5 cup Arborio Rice
  • 3.4 cup white wine
  • 1 quart chicken stock
  • salt and ground black pepper to taste
  • 1/2 cup grated Parmesan cheese

Steps

  1. Press the Saute button on your INSTANT POT. And then add butter and olive oil, stir stir until yellow goo (around 2 minutes will swee swee). Then your mushrooms go in until it soft (have to stir hor), and then onion also stir, rosemary aunty also stir
  2. The rice can go in now. Remember to go far in life you have to do everything good. So u take one rice each and coat with the butter-olive oil mixture until 2 minutes later. Then wine time for 3 minutes. Chicken stock stir in while scarping pot. Simmer for 1 minute liddat
  3. Close and lock the lid like lock-a-lock. Turn the knob to seal, then become high pressure for 6 minutes. But have to wait around 10 to 15 minutes before the pressure become high enough to set the timer for 6 minutes
  4. Tap the knob hello hello with wooden spoon (steel will scratch the knob, plastic will melt). Stand back and then vent your gas. Remove the lid when your gas is all released. Take 5 minutes to remove.
  5. Lastly, finally, stir the risotto until it becomes the creamiest, sizziest, mouth-waterest INSTANT POT MUSHROOM RISOTTO. Throw the rosemary thyme away and season with salt and pepper. Stir in palm and sean cheese until they are one
